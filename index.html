The Critical Problems We're Solving

Hong Kong's food system is broken, but we have the solution

The Paradox

Hong Kong wastes over 3,600 tons of edible food daily while 1 in 4 citizens faces food insecurity - a systemic failure we can no longer ignore.

The System Failure

Current food recycling is complicated, inconvenient, and offers no incentive for participation, causing widespread disengagement.

The Lost Opportunity

We're throwing away perfect nutrition that could feed thousands of families, representing both a moral and economic failure.

Our AI-Powered Rescue Mission

Transforming food waste into community wealth through cutting-edge technology

1

Smart Detection & Data Collection

Our smart bins use AI vision and spectroscopy to instantly identify edible vs. contaminated food, creating a database of waste patterns to optimize collection.

2

AI-Powered Sorting

Advanced AI classifies food waste in real-time, while robotic mechanisms automatically separate valuable nutrition from actual waste with precision.

3

Quality Assurance & Safety

HACCP and ISO 22000 certified safety protocols ensure every rescued meal meets the highest food safety standards before distribution.

4

Centralized Re-processing

Edible food is transformed into nutritious, affordable meals through our certified central kitchen, maximizing nutritional value and taste.

5

Community Distribution

Rescued meals are sold at low cost to grassroots communities, providing dignified access to nutrition while generating sustainable revenue.

6

The Virtuous Cycle

20% of profits are shared with restaurant partners, creating financial incentives that perpetuate the system while funding expansion.

Our Competitive Advantage

The only end-to-end solution transforming food waste into community wealth

Full Vertical Integration

We control the entire chain from collection to plate, eliminating intermediaries and maximizing efficiency while capturing full margin.

AI-Powered Efficiency

Our automated sorting reduces operational costs by 60% compared to manual methods while improving accuracy and scalability.

Win-Win-Win Economics

Restaurants profit from waste, communities access affordable nutrition, and the environment benefits - creating true circular sustainability.

Measurable Impact Across Three Dimensions

Creating positive change that benefits everyone

Environmental Impact

  • Reduce partner restaurant food waste by 70% within first year of implementation
  • Cut methane emissions by 12,000 tons CO2-equivalent annually through landfill diversion
  • Save 450 million liters of water annually from avoided food production waste
  • Protect local biodiversity by reducing demand for new agricultural land expansion

Social Impact

  • Provide 5,000+ affordable nutritious meals daily to low-income families across Hong Kong
  • Free up 15-20% of household income for low-income families to allocate to other essentials
  • Create 120+ green jobs in logistics, AI management, and food safety within first two years
  • Build community solidarity through tangible business-community partnerships

Economic Impact

  • Save restaurants HK$18,000+ monthly in reduced waste disposal fees and tipping costs
  • Generate HK$6.5M annually in shared profits back to restaurant partners through 20% profit sharing
  • Stimulate local green economy with HK$45M+ in economic activity within three years
  • Create scalable model that ensures long-term financial viability without ongoing subsidies
